About

The Reinforcement Learning and Philosophy Workshop (RLPH) is an invitational workshop focused on foundational challenges at the intersection of reinforcement learning and philosophy.

Date & Venue: August 19–21, Mila in Montreal, Canada.

Themes: Philosophy of Mind, Agency, Computation, Artificial Minds.

Organizers
  • John D. Martin (Openmind Research Institute, University of Alberta)
  • Julia Haas (Google DeepMind)
  • Nishanth Anand (McGill)
